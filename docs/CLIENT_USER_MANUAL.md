# CLIENT USER MANUAL
## EmotionAI Pro - Cognitive Enterprise SaaS Platform

---

### Introduction
Welcome to **EmotionAI Pro**, the enterprise platform for cognitive sentiment diagnostics, real-time wellness tracking, and data exports. Follow these simple steps to familiarize yourself with the platform’s core features.

---

### 1. Interactive Application Navigation

The application navigation is organized into **five central tabs** along the unified bottom bar:

```
+-------------------------------------------------------+
|                                                       |
|                                                       |
|               (ACTIVE VIEWPORT CONTENT)               |
|                                                       |
|                                                       |
+-------------------------------------------------------+
|  [>] Analyze | [@@] Analytics | [=] History | [v] Wellness | [*] Profile  |
+-------------------------------------------------------+
```

1.  **Analyze Screen `[>]`**: Real-time evaluation portal. Enter thoughts to view immediate cognitive score breakdowns.
2.  **Analytics Dashboard `[@@]`**: Performance summary metrics. Displays averages, percentages, and history trendlines.
3.  **History Panel `[=]`**: Log storage explorer. Search, review, and analyze historic events.
4.  **Wellness Center `[v]`**: Interactive breathing guide. Standard 4-7-8 deep breathing exercises.
5.  **Profile Control `[*]`**: Account details and custom notifications settings.

---

### 2. Direct Operation Walkthroughs

#### 2.1 User Login & Initial Sign On
*   On first load, you'll see the **Account Access Gate**.
*   If you have registration credentials, type your Username and Password and tap **Authenticate Now**.
*   To create a new account, tap **Don't have an account? Sign Up**, fill in the necessary details, and complete registration.

---

#### 2.2 Analyzing Emotional State
*   Navigate to the **Analyze** tab.
*   Type your current thoughts, feelings, or stress factors into the large text field.
*   The system uses an **800ms debounce typing logic** to analyze your input in real-time. Wait a moment or tap **Analyze Emotional Spectrum** to trigger manual server processing.
*   Review your analyzed status:
    *   **Dominant Emotion Card**: Your primary state and confidence percentage.
    *   **Emotional Score Breakdown**: Multi-colored graphs detailing Happy, Sad, Angry, and Anxiety scores.
    *   **Clinical Recommendations**: Actionable wellbeing tips based on your results.

---

#### 2.3 Reviewing Metrics inside the Dashboard
*   Open the **Analytics** tab.
*   Monitor your **Average Well-being Index** (from 1 to 100).
*   Review the **Dominant Mood Distribution** chart to see your dominant feelings over time.
*   Interact with your **Wellness Trend Timeline** to track week-over-week progress.

---

#### 2.4 Document Auditing & Backup Exports
*   Navigate to the **Profile** tab.
*   Scroll to **System Backup & Exports**.
*   To share a beautiful A4 health report, tap **Export PDF**. To download your complete raw timeline, tap **Export CSV**.
*   Select your preferred sharing option (e.g. email, cloud storage, or printing) from the Android system ShareSheet.

---

#### 2.5 Quick Notification Testing
*   Navigate to the **Profile** tab.
*   Locate the **Diagnostic Notification Testers** panel.
*   Tap **Trigger Daily** or **Trigger Mood** to instantly receive push notifications on your device.
